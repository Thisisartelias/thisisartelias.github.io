FAQ

Common questions about the artwork and commissions.

Questions

Everything people often ask.

Do you take custom commission requests?

\

Yes, I take custom commission requests. More information about that at the "Commissions" page.

What sizes are available for commissioned paintings?

Basicly every size, as long as it's shippable.

Can I request specific colors or a certain mood for the piece?

Yes, you can request any color/theme.

How long does a commission usually take to finish?

Depends very much on size and if I have the matarials in stock. It ususally takes a couple of weeks to finish.

Are the paintings on the gallery page original one of one works?

Yes, they are all original one of one and handmade. No copies/prints.

Can I ask about a painting that is already shown in the gallery?

Of course! I'm happy to help with any question about my art/the process.

Do you ship paintings outside the Netherlands?

Yes, but only on request (within the EU only.)

What is the best way to start a commission?

The best way to start your commission is to fill in the contact form, or email to ContactArtElias@gmail.com
