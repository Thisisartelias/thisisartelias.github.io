F.A.Q.

Answers to common questions so you don't have to email me about them.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. Small pieces are shipped as-is. Works larger than 100cm are removed from their stretcher bars, rolled, and shipped in heavy-duty tubes to prevent damage and keep shipping costs reasonable. You will need to have it re-stretched at a local framing shop.

Can I ask for a specific subject in a commission?

Not really. My work is non-representational. If you want a portrait of your dog, I am not the right artist for you. If you want a painting that feels like the memory of a storm, we can talk.

What materials do you use?

Primarily heavy body acrylics because they dry fast and allow for aggressive layering. I also use oil sticks, charcoal, graphite, spray paint, and occasionally household construction materials like plaster or joint compound for texture.

Are the edges of the canvas painted?

Yes. I paint messy and the paint inevitably wraps around the edges. I consider the edges part of the piece. They do not need to be framed, though you can if you prefer.

When will your music be released on Spotify/Apple Music?

The debut single is in final mixing now. Check the RELEASES timeline page for the exact drop dates. It will be available on all major streaming platforms.
