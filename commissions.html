The Process

1

Consultation

We discuss size, vibe, and colors. No strict sketches provided due to the abstract nature.

2

Deposit

A 50% non-refundable deposit secures your spot in the queue and covers materials.

3

Creation

I paint. Usually takes 3-6 weeks. I'll send one mid-point update.

4

Delivery

Final approval, remaining 50% paid, and the piece is shipped.

Base Pricing

  • Small (40x40cm to 60x60cm)€300 - €500
  • Medium (80x80cm to 100x100cm)€650 - €900
  • Large (120x100cm to 150x120cm)€1,200 - €1,800
  • Massive / Custom ShapesUpon Request

Prices do not include shipping. Shipping large canvases internationally requires rolling the canvas and shipping in a tube, requiring re-stretching locally.
