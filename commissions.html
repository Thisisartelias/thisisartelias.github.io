Process

How a commission works

Get in touch with your idea, preferred size, colors, timeline, and budget. We will go through your wishes together before I begin.

Once the plan is clear, I make a draft. If you are happy with it, I start the painting. A deposit is requested for material costs, and I share progress updates during the process.

Availability

Shipping and delivery.

Shipping is available within the Netherlands. EU delivery can also be arranged on request.

For the most accurate quote, please contact me directly so we can discuss the details of the piece.
