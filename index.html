About

A young artist with a clear point of view.

I'm Elias, 13 years old, from the Netherlands. I make and love abstract art, and this site is a place to present that work in a more professional way.

My paintings focus on color, movement, and feeling. Some pieces are bold and energetic, others are more minimal, but each one is designed to feel original and personal.
